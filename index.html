Welcome to my personal page.

Below is an interactive visualisation of London House Prices that I created for the Imperial Summer Data Challenge back in 2014.
The input data was a list of house purchases made between 2009 and May 2014 along with their attributes, e.g. whether they were flats, newbuilds, freehold or leasehold, etc...
The London borough boundary data was obtained from http://data.london.gov.uk/.
The map was created with d3.js and a little bit of R to reformat the data.
Apologies in advance that this page is not responsive!
